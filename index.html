About YOLOcon

Eastern Michigan University's Information Assurance Student Association (IASA) is pleased to announce the fourth annual Ypsilanti Online Learning Opportunity Conference - YOLOCon 20 on Saturday, April 4th, 2020!

The objective of this conference is to inspire students to practice their abilities interfacing with people, businesses, and presenting themselves in a professional manner. Student led talks will cover a range of information from offensive red team to live forensics. All topics are student selected, and focused on what facet of Information Assurance, or technology, that each student is individually passionate about.

The conference will be hosted in 1st Floor Roosevelt Hall, at 698 W Forest Ave #626, Ypsilanti, MI 48197.
